DESCRIPTION:

The design is inspired by nature.

I like simple things that don't look monotonous. For example, nature has an ever-changing and rich appearance. I use various leaves and vines in the font design to design a font that surrounds the elements of nature and is closer to the beauty of nature.

In addition to this, I also added some small red flowers that look inconspicuous but essential to this theme. Because this will keep the design from looking boring. Moreover, the whole design will appear very close to nature, and there will not be too many colors to make people feel comfortable.
